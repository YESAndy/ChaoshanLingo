# Jieyang course policy

This course targets Jieyang Teochew (揭阳话, Gek-ion), using urban Jieyang/Rongcheng as the reference variety unless a skill identifies another local source.

## Writing and pronunciation

- Romanization is Guangdong Peng'im with Jieyang pronunciation.
- Tone numbers are citation tones in vocabulary. Native recordings must provide the spoken tone-sandhi form in phrases.
- Chinese characters are a reading aid, not a guarantee that every Teochew word has a standard Mandarin-equivalent character.
- Do not silently merge Shantou, Chaozhou, Jieyang, and diaspora forms. Record variants in comments or `Also accepted` only after native-speaker review.

## Review status

The source material is a starting point, not a substitute for a Jieyang speaker. Before release, review every word, character, translation, and recording with a native speaker from the target area. In particular, check `le2/leu2`, vowel correspondences, local tone values, and tone sandhi.
